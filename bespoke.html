The Process

How it works

あなただけの一点ものができるまで

01

Consultation

Share your vision, inspirations, and budget. We listen deeply to understand what the piece means to you.

想い、インスピレーション、ご予算をお聞かせください。

02

Design

We translate your story into sketches and material proposals. Revisions continue until the design feels right.

スケッチと素材提案で形にします。納得いくまで何度でも。

03

Crafting

Your piece is hand-forged in our Kuramae atelier. We share progress updates throughout the making.

蔵前の工房で手作業で制作。進捗は随時お知らせします。

04

Delivery

Final inspection under natural light, then carefully packaged and delivered to you. A piece born from conversation.

自然光で最終検品後、丁寧に梱包してお届けします。
